Used car price prediction dataset
Predict a car's resale price from its listing details
input features
Vehicle identity
Usage and history
Mechanical
Appearance
Feature Type What it represents
brand Text Manufacturer, e.g. Ford, Audi, Tesla
model Text Specific model and trim name
model_year Year Year the car was manufactured
milage Miles Total distance the car has driven
accident Category Whether an accident or damage was reported
clean_title Category Whether the car has a clean title
fuel_type Category Gasoline, hybrid, diesel, electric, etc.
engine Text Horsepower, size, and cylinder count
transmission Category Gearbox type, e.g. 8-speed automatic
ext_col Category Exterior paint color
int_col Category Interior color
output
price
The car's resale price at listing time
Type: regression
Predicting a continuous dollar value, not a class
Models tried: linear regression and random forest, evaluated with MAE and R2
All these features describe one car listing. The model combines them to predict the output: how much that car is worth.
